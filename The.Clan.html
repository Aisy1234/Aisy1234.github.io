Goliath

Goliath is the leader of the Manhattan Clan. He is a strong and intelligent warrior, with a firm sense of morality coupled with a short temper. He takes his role as a protector seriously, and will willingly put his life in danger to keep New York or his clan safe from harm. He also develops a relationship with Elisa Maza.

Elisa Maza

Elisa is a police detective, and the closest human friend of the clan. She helps them to adapt to the modern world, and soon becomes the love interest of Goliath.

Brooklyn

Brooklyn is a young but rebellious teenager of the clan. He likes exploring and finding out more about the human ways. He is also very quick-witted and has a certain fondness for making wisecracks at times. He later becomes Goliath's second-in-command.

Broadway

Broadway is the jolly and occasionally naive member of the clan with a fondness for food, as well as Westerns and detective movies. He is loyal, reliable, friendly and quite intelligent despite his appearance, and was the main focus of several of the show's more thought-provoking episodes.

Lexington

Lexington is the technical specialist of the clan. He is eager, analytical and outgoing, but somewhat impatient. He is also quite a capable engineer, as he had once built a motorcycle for Brooklyn. He also has a knack for computers.

Hudson

Hudson is the eldest of the clan, previous leader, and Goliath's mentor. He often gets baffled by the modern ways of the new world, and holds onto the past. Though old aged, he gives advice to his fellow gargoyles when they're having problems.

Bronx

Bronx is the watchdog of the clan, and, while having no wings like the others, possesses great speed and agility. He is also an excellent judge of character. He also hates pidgeons with a burning passion.

Angela

Angela is Goliath and Demona's biological daughter, raised on the island of Avalon after the Wyvern massacre. After Goliath, Elisa, and Bronx help her and her clan defeat the Archmage, she returns to New York with them to join the clan. She possesses her father's temper, but has a kind nature.
